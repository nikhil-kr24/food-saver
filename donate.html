🙏 Why Donate?

Your surplus food can feed families in need. Here's how to make your donation count:

⏱️
List food as early as possible — NGOs need time to arrange pickup.
📞
Keep your phone accessible after listing. NGOs may call to coordinate.
📦
Pack food securely in containers to maintain hygiene and freshness.
🗺️
Provide a precise address with landmarks for easy navigation.
🌡️
Keep cooked food covered and at safe temperature until collection.
